History

The inaugural summer school coincided with the SUITMA 9 international congress organized by RUDN University in May 2017. The next 3MUGIS- 2018 included an intensive week of in-door an out-door lectures, master-classes, field work and informal discussion with the top level scientists and practioners, followed by a week-long field tour observing natural and anthropogenic soils and landscapes of Central Russia. In 2019, an innovative format of the school expanded the list of studied topics and geographical horizons of the field trip. Five days of on-campus training were followed by a two-week field tour from the Barents Sea to the Azov Sea and from subarctic to the dry steppe landscapes.
