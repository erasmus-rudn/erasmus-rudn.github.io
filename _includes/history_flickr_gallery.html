History

For the first time, the school was organized in Moscow ahead of the SUITMA 9 international congress in May 2017 and attracted students and speakers from 12 countries. According to the SUITMA (Soils of urban, industrial, traffic, mining and military areas) - the professional community of urban soil scientist - this was the first some school, addressing an issue of urban soils at a broad content and at the international level
