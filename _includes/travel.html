>Visa support

Normally every foreign visitor should apply for a Russian Visa to enter Russia except the citizens of Azerbaijan, Armenia, Belorussia, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Moldova, Tajikistan and Uzbekistan. Citizens of Argentina, Brazil, Venezuela, Israel, Colombia and Chile can enter Russia without a visa for the period of 90 days. Citizens of Cuba, Thailand, Serbia (special requirements for passport), Montenegro and Ecuador can stay in Russia without a visa for up to 30 days.

Conference operator MESOL LLC can help you with the necessary documents to receive Russian tourist visa.

You can indicate an information about the necessity in visa support documents while filling in the registration form or just inform the coordinator of the Conference by e-mail stas@mesol.ru

If you book a room through Conference operator MESOL LLC, the visa support documents will be cost 500 RUB. Please be informed that for citizens of some countries (for example China, India) the visa support documents will be more expensive + the Russian consulates in their countries can require the original of the documents.

Visa support documents:

  • Tourist voucher
  • Foreign tourist reception confirmation

To provide you with visa support documents we need the copy of your passport (with the validity exceeding the planned date of departure from Russia by 6 month) and the whole period of your stay in Russia (otherwise the visa support documents will be provided only for the period of booking a room through Conference operator MESOL LLC).

We recommend you to visit the website of Russian Embassy (Consulate) of your residence country and check the list of the documents you need to prepare.

Generally they are:

  1. Passport (should be valid no less than 6 months after the expiry date of the visa you are applying for)
  2. 1 or 2 photos
  3. Application form for Russian tourist visa (please note in the application form that your purpose of visit is tourism or aim tourism)
  4. Tourist voucher
  5. Foreign tourist reception confirmation
  6. Confirmation of the hotel reservation.
  7. Medical insurance

In application form for Russian tourist visa you will need to indicate:

  • Purpose of visit – tourism / aim tourism (Please be informed!!! If you mention that you are going to the Conference the Russian Consulate will ask you to apply for business visa. In this case you will need to mention that your purpose of visit is TOURISM)
  • Name of Host company – Visa Centre, LLC
  • Address of the host company - Leninsky Avenue, 153 А, office 706, St. Petersburg, 191186
  • Reference number - 007612
  • Confirmation # = number of the tourist voucher

The visa support documents will be provided only after the payment of the registration fee and visa support documents / post delivery if necessary
